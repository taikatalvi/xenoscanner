PRIVACY POLICY

XenoScanner · Last updated: 13 July 2026

XenoScanner ("the app") is designed to run entirely on your device. This policy explains what the app accesses and confirms that it does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

SUMMARY

XenoScanner does not collect any data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no network transmission of your information.

CAMERA AND MOTION SENSORS

The app uses your device's camera, LiDAR/depth sensor (on supported devices), and motion sensors solely to power its real-time scanning features (motion radar and LiDAR wireframe).

  • All sensor data is processed live, on your device.
  • Nothing from the camera, depth sensor, or motion sensors is recorded, saved to your photo library, or transmitted anywhere.
  • The app requests camera access only so it can display and analyze the live feed while you are using it.

DATA COLLECTION

We do not collect, log, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, contacts, etc.)
  • Photos, video, or audio recordings
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Advertising or tracking identifiers

THIRD PARTIES

The app contains no third-party SDKs, no advertising networks, and no analytics services. No data is shared with anyone.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
